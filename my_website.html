I am Xinyu Wu. I am now a Ph.D. student in Laboratory and Decision Systems (LIDS) at MIT, advised by Prof. Eytan Modiano. I am in the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics and also in the Interdisciplinary Doctoral Program in Statistics (IDPS). My research interests include robustness, control, and optimization over dynamical network systems.

I obtained my B.S. degree in Electronic Engineering and a minor in Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During the undergraduate, I did research in the Intelligent Internet of Things (IIoT) lab, over social network de-anonymization and wireless fingerprints prediction for positioning, instructed by Prof. Luoyi Fu, Prof. Xiaohua Tian, and Prof. Xinbing Wang.
